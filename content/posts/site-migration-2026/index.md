This site has been fully rebuilt and expanded. Here's what happened.

## WordPress to Hugo

The original **uo.wzk.cz** ran on WordPress since 2009, hosting 17 UO tool pages with 27 download files. In March 2026, the entire site was migrated to [Hugo](https://gohugo.io/) (a static site generator) and deployed on [Cloudflare Pages](https://pages.cloudflare.com/). All original content, downloads, and URLs have been preserved.

Why the move? WordPress was overkill for what is essentially a frozen archive — no comments, no dynamic content, just static tool pages and downloads. Hugo builds the entire site in under a second, costs nothing to host, and will keep working for years without maintenance.

## Manawydan Archive Rescued

The bigger news: **ultima.manawydan.cz** (originally at eranova.cz) has been merged into this site.

For those who don't know — RadstaR ran a comprehensive UO tools archive from 2004 to 2016, collecting editors, convertors, server emulators, and tutorials for Ultima Online development. It was the go-to Czech resource for UO modding. The site was hosted by [Manawydan.cz](http://www.manawydan.cz/) (a Czechoslovak UO shard) and later went offline when the hosting expired.

**Golfin** from the UO Erebor community had cached the entire Manawydan archive on his servers, preserving it from being lost. In March 2026, we recovered this cache — 176 MB of tools, screenshots, tutorials, and downloads — and merged it into uo.wzk.cz.

## What's New

The merged archive now contains:

- **107 tool and tutorial pages** (up from 17)
- **~200 download files** totaling ~200 MB (up from 27 files)
- **4 Czech-language tutorials** by RadstaR on creating items, animations, buildings, and working with MUL/verdata files
- **Server emulators**: RunUO (versions 1.0 through 2.6), RunUO RE, SunUO, GemUO
- **New tool categories**: Server, UOKR/SA, Tutorials
- **Author attribution**: Tools are tagged by their creators — RadstaR, Arya, Orbsydia, Punt, Kons, Ravenal, VD

Tools from the Manawydan archive are marked with a green **MW** badge throughout the site.
